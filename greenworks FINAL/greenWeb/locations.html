Recycling Centers Near You

Use our map to make a change today!

Every city or town have their own recycling methods but most are required to have recycling collection and center operation. Look into your city's recycling rules and regulations to know their guidelines and what kinds of products they recycle. Recyclable plastics are usually given to recycling centers in order to be processed, breaked down into raw materials, and manufactured into new products. Before you recycle, here's three rules Waste Management, or WM, have for recycling:

  • Recycle clean bottles, cans, paper, and cardboard
  • Keep food and liquid out of your recycling
  • No loose plastic bags and no bagged recyclables.

The map above displays all recycling centers near you - no need to search it up yourself! Just keep in mind that, as mentioned before, for each plastic there can be different recycling centers. If you cannot find a recycling center that accepts the plastic product you have, contact your local stores and determine if they have their own recycling programs. The best thing we can do as consumers is to throw away as little as possible. Incorporating small things into daily life like getting your own container for foods and beverages can help - check out our Live Sustainably Page for more alternatives to wasting plastic. We appreciate you participating in helping the world become a healthier, cleaner place to live!
