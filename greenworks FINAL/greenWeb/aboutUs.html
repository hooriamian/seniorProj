Our world is a beautiful place and has been sustaining life for millions of year. However, our presence is damaging it gravely - we still have a chance to change it.

Living next to the beach, Monmouth residents and commuters should realize the impact of pollution on the ocean and the overall environment. Each year, the Youth Activist Club hosts a beach cleanup and there isn't any shortage of garbage for members to clean. Many students aren't aware of how their small daily decisions can make a huge negative impact on the evironment. For example, hundreds of students visit the Dunkin Donuts for their daily coffee and use the plastic cups each time. Our task is to encourage them to switch to reusable cups that can help save so much plastic and cause a positive change on the environment!

Our main goal going into this project was to ensure that it was going to be for a good cause. Our concern for the environment is linked to the love for our planet. We have to make a change and take action NOW in order to have any hope on saving the planet. Together, Hooria, Davina, Katie, Jackie, Alyssa, and Sabina decided that we would utilize the opportunity of creating something innovative for our senior project but also get our message out to our viewers. We are all also individual activists who plan to host our very own clean up projects in order to save the planet. We believe that every little action counts towards a brighter future for the planet Earth, our home.
