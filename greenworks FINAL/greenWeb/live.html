Live Sustainably

Here are some awesome alternatives to help you live more sustainably!


Alternatives for 1 PET:

  • Hydro Flask
  • Lifefactory Glass Bottle
  • Klean Kanteen
  • Cayman Insulated Water Bottle
  • Boxed Water
  • Insulated Stainless Steel Bottles


Alternatives for 2 HDPE:

  • Switch to purchasing milk and oil products in glass bottles
  • Canvas Shopping Bags
  • Reusable Cloth Shopping Bags
  • Mesh Shopping Bags


Alternatives for 3 PVC:

  • Replace plastic food wrap with reusable beeswax wraps
  • Replace plastic toys with reclaimed wool stuffed animals
  • Replace PVC garden hose with a Drinking Water Safe Garden Hose
  • Use mason jars and metal containers to store dry foods


Alternatives for 4 LDPE:

  • Replace plastic grocery bags with fabric alternatives
  • Take cloth bags to local bakery
  • Replace plastic sandwich bags with platinum silicone alternatives
  • Use mason jars to store food


Alternatives for 5 PP:

  • Reusable straws
  • Reusable water bottles
  • Cloth diapers


Alternatives for 6 PS

  • Reusable coffee cup
  • Compostable or reusable picnic cutlery
  • Bamboo utensils
  • Bring your own takeaway containers
  • Stainless steel containers


Alternatives for 7 Other:

  • Products made with platinum silicone
  • Products made of glass
  • Products made with stainless steel
  • Products make with natural fiber
